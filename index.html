Purpose

About

The APSA South Regional Conference is an APSA-sponsored conference designed to mentorship and collaboration for those interest in physician scientist careers.

Who Should Attend?

This conference welcomes all undergrads, postbacs, MD/PhD, DO/PhD, MD, DO, and health sciences graduate programs who are interested in learning about physician scientist careers. Residents, fellows, postdocs, and faculty are also welcome to attend.

Abstracts and Posters

Abstract submission will open in July and extend until [].

Everyone is welcome to submit abstracts for poster sessions. Posters are automatically entered into a judging competition with cash prizes. Poster categories include []. Everyone who submits an abstract is required to register for the conference.

Abstract word length: 300 words maximum

Poster sizing: try to keep your poster within 48 x 36 inches in size.

Accommodations

Top-scoring abstracts will be awarded a travel stipend and a refund for registration costs based on a need-based scholarship.
